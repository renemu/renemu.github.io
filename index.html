My Projects

This is a 16-channel relay board with 2 selectors to read each of the 8 relay channel sections, with indicators in the form of green led lights for the selectors and red led lights for the 8 relay section selectors.

Monitoring the voltage of a 1.2V ni-cd cell battery with a cell reader controlled by an Arduino MEGA 2560 to a relay selector.

The monitoring performed will be displayed on the screen in the form of cell number (#), cell voltage (V), cell status (!), total cell voltage, balancing status, cell charge current, and teg status.

The cell battery condition is also sent to the internet as remote monitoring to make it easier for users when cell battery monitoring takes place.
